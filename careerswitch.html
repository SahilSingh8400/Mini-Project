Suggested Next Steps for Career Switch

  • Identify transferable skills from your current career.
  • Research industries related to your new interest.
  • Learn new skills through online courses or certifications.
  • Connect with professionals already working in that field.
  • Start with small projects, internships, or part-time roles.
